B787 ECL Emulator

Installation:

iOS: In Safari, tap on the share icon, then Add to Home Screen.

Android: In Chrome, tap the three dot icon on the top, then Add to Home Screen.

Launch the app at least once before going offline. It should then work even when offline.

The Goal:

For now, it's just a glorified table of contents. The actual checklists are not yet implemented. Use it to simply train your muscle memory to find checklists quickly and efficiently.
