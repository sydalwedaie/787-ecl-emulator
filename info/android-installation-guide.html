--- layout: app-info title: ANDROID INSTALLATION GUIDE ---

STEP 1: Open app in Google Chrome (app link)

Skip to the next step if you are already in Chrome on a portable Android device.

STEP 2: Wait for the install prompt to appear. This may take up to a few minutes, depending on your internet speed.

STEP 3:Tap Install.

STEP 4: The web page is added as a standalone app in your app drawer.
